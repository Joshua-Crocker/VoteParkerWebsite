Hello, College of the North Atlantic community!

I'm thrilled to introduce myself as a candidate for the presidency of our beloved institution. My journey at CNA has been nothing short of transformative, and I am now committed to taking our college to new heights as your advocate and leader.

Listening to Your Concerns:

At the heart of my campaign is an unwavering commitment to you, the students. I understand that the strength of our college lies in its diverse and vibrant student body. That's why I am dedicated to listening to your concerns, no matter how big or small they may seem. Your experiences, ideas, and challenges matter to me, and I promise to be a president who actively seeks out your voices and brings your concerns to the forefront of our college's decision-making process.

Bringing Student Issues to Faculty:

A thriving academic institution is one where students and faculty work hand-in-hand to create an environment of excellence. As your president, I will bridge the gap between students and faculty by championing open and constructive dialogue. Your academic needs and concerns will not go unheard, as I will be your advocate, ensuring that faculty and administrators are aware of and responsive to the issues that matter most to you.

Community Outreach and Engagement:

College of the North Atlantic is not just a place of learning; it's a cornerstone of our community. I believe in the power of education to transform lives and strengthen communities. Together, we will bolster our college's community outreach efforts, forging stronger bonds with local organizations, businesses, and individuals. By collaborating with the broader community, we can provide invaluable opportunities for personal and academic growth while giving back to the region that supports us.

A Commitment to All Students:

Every student at CNA deserves to feel valued, heard, and cared for. I am deeply committed to ensuring that every student, regardless of background or circumstance, receives the support they need to thrive. Our college should be a place where everyone can reach their full potential, and I will work tirelessly to make this vision a reality.


In the spirit of inclusivity and progress, I ask for your support, your ideas, and your trust. Together, we can build a brighter future for College of the North Atlantic, one where student voices are heard, community bonds are strengthened, and all students are given the opportunity to succeed. Join me on this journey to make our college the best it can be!


Thank you for considering me as your advocate and candidate for the presidency of College of the North Atlantic. Together, we can make a lasting impact and create a college experience that we can all be proud of.
